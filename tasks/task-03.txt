Create the complete data file containing all 43 kings with their full structured data, plus the 3 era definitions, plus helper functions for querying the data. This is the largest data entry task and the heart of the application.

DEPENDENCIES

WHAT OTHER TASKS NEED FROM THIS

Task 10 (Quiz Engine) uses KINGS to generate questions, and uses FactCategory on keyFacts to pick smart distractors.
Task 12 (Quiz Page) uses the data for the global quiz.
